Objective

Predict whether a pause corresponds to End-of-Turn (EOT) or Hold using only causal information before pause_start. No future audio or pause end timestamps are used.

Engineering Timeline

  1. Baseline – constant predictor.
  2. Prosodic features – energy and pitch statistics.
  3. MFCC features – added 26 spectral descriptors.
  4. HistGradientBoosting – rejected after detecting overfitting.
  5. StandardScaler + Logistic Regression – selected as final model.

Inference Pipeline

Audio → 1.5 s Window → Energy + Pitch + MFCC → StandardScaler → Logistic Regression → P(EOT)
